Welcome and thank you for visiting Sew Rose online.


Finding the perfect outfit can be tricky. Ensuring you get a perfect fit can be even more of a challenge, particularly if you’re not the fashion industry’s idea of an average size or shape.


But the perfect fit isn’t impossible. Sew Rose offers a range of services for men, women and children, from alterations to re-styles and made-to-measure garments.


Services include:

  1. •Hemming or shortening

  2. •Lengthening

  3. •Taking in or out

  4. •Strap shortening

  5. •New zips

  6. •Raising waistlines

  7. •Replacing lining

  8. •General mending/darning

  9. •Tailoring (upon consultation)

  10. •Modernising past seasons’ fashions

  11. •Occasion wear e.g. Christening and wedding gowns
