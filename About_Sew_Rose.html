Sew Rose is an alterations and tailoring business established by Rose Abbott at her home in Harpenden, Hertfordshire, and now relocating to the Medway Towns, Kent


Rose has over 35 years’ experience working in couture fashion and the clothing industry. She began her career with the Queen’s official dressmaker Hardy Amies in Savile Row in 1970. After several years learning the trade, Rose left to work closer to home and joined women’s clothing company Eastex, now part of Alexon Group.


Several years later, Rose left Eastex to bring up her young family. During this time, she worked with tailors and formal-wear clothes shops in Bedfordshire, including Anthony Stewart of Luton.


Most recently Rose has worked with a number of women’s fashion boutiques in Harpenden, including designer clothes boutique TurnStyle.
